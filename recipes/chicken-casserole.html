Potluck Chicken Casserole

Description

This chicken tender Parmesan casserole is a one-dish version of a favorite, with frozen, breaded chicken tenders for simplicity. It's a hearty family dinner with minimal cleanup.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 8 ounces penne, or other small pasta
  • 3 cups marinara sauce, such as Rao's®, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken bone broth
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, torn (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 16 frozen breaded chicken tenders, such as Just Bare®
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Place penne in the casserole dish. Pour 2 cups marinara sauce over the pasta. Add chicken bone broth and gently stir to combine.
  3. Sprinkle in half of the spinach (if using), Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cover tightly with foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until pasta is tender and most liquid is absorbed, 25 to 30 minutes.
  5. Remove casserole from oven. Stir in remaining spinach until wilted. Arrange frozen chicken tenders evenly on top of the pasta. Spoon remaining sauce over chicken, just enough to cover lightly. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan evenly over the top.
  6. Remove casserole from oven. Stir in remaining spinach until wilted. Arrange frozen chicken tenders evenly on top of the pasta. Spoon remaining sauce over chicken, just enough to cover lightly. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan evenly over the top.
